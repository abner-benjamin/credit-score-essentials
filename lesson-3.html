Real-Life Decisions, Real Credit Impact

Now that you’ve learned what a credit score is and what affects it, let’s apply that knowledge. Each situation below is based on common real-world financial decisions. Read each carefully and choose the best answer. Then, check to see how your decision could affect your score.

Scenario 1: Applying for New Credit

You apply for three new credit cards in the same week because they all offer welcome bonuses.

Scenario 2: Late Payment

You forget to pay your credit card bill. It’s now 29 days late, but you plan to pay it tomorrow.

Scenario 3: Closing a Paid-Off Card

You just paid off one of your credit cards and decide to close the account to reduce temptation
